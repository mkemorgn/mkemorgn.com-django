Detroit 2020

Went to Detroit to see a Larry June show. It was March 2020. As we were driving to Detroit we were receiving news of the first COVID-19 outbreaks in PA/ Allegheny County.

We began to assume the worst but we were already committed so we continued on. Upon arriving in Detroit it was clear we weren't seeing a concert. It looked like they were already under lock down conditions.

We made the best of the trip anyways.
